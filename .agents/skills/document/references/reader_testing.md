# Reader Testing

Reader testing checks whether the draft works for someone who lacks the author's background context.

## Method

1. Generate concrete reader questions from the draft.
2. Try to answer them using only the draft.
3. Mark each question as pass or fail.
4. Fix the sections responsible for failed questions.

## Good reader questions

- What problem is this document trying to solve?
- What is being proposed or decided?
- Why was this option chosen over the alternatives?
- What risks or tradeoffs matter most?
- What should happen next?

## Failure patterns

- The summary uses terms the body never defines.
- The body explains implementation details but never states the decision clearly.
- The draft says what to do but not why.
- The draft assumes prior knowledge of an incident, system, or team convention.
- The next action or owner is missing.
